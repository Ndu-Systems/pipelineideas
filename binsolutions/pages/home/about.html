About Us

Pipeline ideas buildings and projects is a service rendering business that offers building repairs and maintenance, renovation work, corporate cleaning services and bin cleaning services with offices in Soweto, Johannesburg. Pipeline ideas buildings and projects plans to become a leader in the services it provides by offering holistic facility management services that are high quality to every client. The company’s overall strategy is based on continued improvement by setting goals, implementing them, measuring results and better utilizing received feedback for improving service delivery through taking calculated risks to facilitate further growth.

Pipeline Ideas Buildings and Projects’ was founded in December 2012 in terms of section 14 of the Companies Act 2008. We are a 135% BBBEE compliant. The company is registered with NHBRC and CIDB board in 6 classes of work. We are also a member of the NCCA for cleaning services.
