ABOUT PIPELINE IDEAS BUILDINGS & PROJECTS

Pipeline Ideas Buildings and Projects offers maintenance and general building services of commercial and residential buildings. Its capabilities encompass the full range of provision of occupational health and safety consultancy, general and health care risk waste management services and food safety i.e. meat, poultry, dairy and other perishables. The diversity of services rendered and goods supply enable us to meet the demands of the growing business world both in the public and private sector.

Building repairs and maintenance, Waste management, Grass cutting and road cleaning !

Sanele Gabela is the sole owner of Pipeline Ideas Buildings and Projects which was founded in December 2012 in terms of section 14 of the Companies Act 2008. Pipeline Ideas Buildings and Projects is a 100% BEE compliant i.e. level 1 company. The company is registered with NHBRC and CIDB board in the category: 2GB; 2SO; 1EB; 1CE; 1SN; 1SF, 1ME Class of work. We are also a member of the NCCA for cleaning services.
